Privacy Policy for AL Life Assistant

Last updated: April 2026

AL Life Assistant (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be processed when you use the AL Life Assistant mobile application.

1. Information We May Process

When you use the application, the following information may be processed:

  • Text Input: messages, prompts, and written requests you submit.
  • Voice Input: audio or microphone input used for voice features.
  • Image Uploads: photos or images you choose to upload for analysis.
  • Device / Technical Data: basic technical data such as device type, operating system, diagnostics, crash logs, and performance data.

2. How We Use Information

We may use processed information to:

  • Provide AI-generated responses and assistant features.
  • Enable chat, voice, and image analysis functions.
  • Improve app stability, quality, and performance.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, spam, or misuse.
  • Maintain service security and reliability.

3. Permissions

The app may request permissions such as:

  • Microphone: used only for voice features.
  • Photos / Media Access: used only when you choose to upload an image.
  • Internet Access: used to connect to online AI and backend services.

You can manage permissions through your device settings.

4. Data Storage and Retention

We aim to minimize data retention. Voice, text, and images may be processed temporarily to provide requested features.

  • We do not intentionally store personal conversations permanently.
  • Temporary logs may be retained for diagnostics, security, abuse prevention, or reliability.
  • Some third-party providers may process data according to their own policies when required to provide the service.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers, including AI service providers, hosting providers, analytics, infrastructure, or security services. These providers may process limited data only to provide requested functionality and operate the application.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7. AI Limitations

AL Life Assistant uses artificial intelligence. Responses may sometimes be inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, or inappropriate for your situation. Users should independently verify important information.

8. No Professional Advice

AL Life Assistant provides general informational assistance only. It does not provide medical, legal, financial, mental health, emergency, engineering, or other professional advice.

Always consult qualified professionals for important decisions.

9. Emergency Use

This application is not intended for emergency or crisis situations. If you are in danger or need urgent help, contact your local emergency services or qualified professionals immediately.

10. Acceptable Use

Users may not use the app for unlawful, abusive, harmful, fraudulent, violent, exploitative, or malicious purposes.

We may refuse responses, limit functionality, or restrict access when necessary to prevent misuse or protect safety.

11. Children

AL Life Assistant is not directed toward children under the applicable minimum legal age in your region without parental or guardian supervision.

12. Your Choices

You may:

  • Stop using the app at any time.
  • Revoke permissions through your device settings.
  • Contact us regarding privacy questions or data-related requests.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a revised “Last updated” date.
